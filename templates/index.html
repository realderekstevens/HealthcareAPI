I'm Derek Stevens


Your Medicare Advisor

Derek is a field advisor specializing in Medicare Supplement Plans, Medicare Advantage Plans, Medicare Part D Plans, Ancillary Plans - Hospital Indemnity, Cancer, Heart, Dental & Vision, Life Insurance, Long Term Care and Annuities.

He gives clients an unfair advantage in retirement planning by talking with them about their structure and conducting a side-by-side comparison of all the options available to them. "My goal is to provide every client peace-of-mind by minimizing burdens and maximizing entitlements." He has a proven track record of helping hundreds of clients audit their policies.

He also has a lot of information on long-term care and 403(b) plans. "I know a lot about healthcare expenses because I used to bill for them." He is also an active member of several 403(b) hospital retirement plans and the Colorado teacher retirement PERA plan.

Derek grew up in Taos, New Mexico. He studied economics at the University of New Mexico while working as nurse aid. He finished his undergrad studies then obtained K-12 teaching certifications from Adams State Colorado. Derek is currently licensed to practice in AZ, CA, FL, MI, NM, NY, OH, SC, TX, WV.

When not advocating for clients Derek is an avid mountain climber. He currently resides in Tucson, Arizona.
